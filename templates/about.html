About Goldwings Aviation

Our Story

Goldwings Aviation was established in 2025. We offer a boutique flight school experience, focused on quality and personalised service. Here, you're not just one of hundreds—you’re part of our community from day one.

Meet Our Chief Flight Instructor

Our Chief Instructor, Muhammad Khalid, began his flying career in 2015, flying out of Point Cook. Throughout his journey from RPL to PPL and CPL, flying across Victoria, Khalid discovered the importance of high-quality instruction and meaningful mentoring.

Khalid is a senior flight instructor with hundreds of hours of teaching experience. He can guide you from your very first lesson all the way to your PPL flight test. He’s driven by a genuine passion for helping students succeed and achieve their goals.

Flight Training We Offer

We currently offer RPL and PPL training in our ownCessna 172, with flexible lesson times to fit around your school, work, or family commitments.

If you’ve ever dreamed of flying, Contact us today to learn more or book a school tour.

Why Train at Moorabbin Airport with Goldwings Aviation

Moorabbin Airport is home to one of the largest aviation education precincts in the world. With five operational runways, 24-hour access, and air traffic control services, it offers students a real-world flying experience to fly in both fixed-wing aircraft or rotary aircraft.

Located just 20km from Melbourne CBD, Moorabbin provides the perfect place to learn to fly. "If you can fly here, you can fly anywhere."

Goldwings Aviation is conveniently located right next to the Moorabbin Airport control tower, providing quick acess to runways and airside operations. Students benefit from our dedicated learning environment, added services like a courtesy collection service from the pilot shop and direct access to our training aircraft parked just steps away from the classroom.

We understand your time is valuable, the Goldwings Aviation facilities ensures a smooth, efficient training experience from ground briefings to takeoff.
