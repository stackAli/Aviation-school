About Goldwings Aviation

Our Story

Goldwings Aviation was established in 2025. We offer a boutique flight school experience, focused on quality and personalised service. Here, you're not just one of hundreds—you’re part of our community from day one.

Meet Our Chief Flight Instructor

Our Chief InstructorMuhammad Khalidbegan his flying career in 2015, flying out of Point Cook. Throughout his journey from RPL to PPL and CPL, flying across Victoria, Khalid discovered the importance of high-quality instruction and meaningful mentoring.

Khalid is a senior flight instructor with hundreds of hours of teaching experience. He can guide you from your very first lesson all the way to your PPL flight test. He’s driven by a genuine passion for helping students succeed and achieve their goals.

Flight Training We Offer

We currently offer RPL and PPL training in our ownCessna 172, with flexible lesson times to fit around your school, work, or family commitments.

If you’ve ever dreamed of flying, Contact us today to learn more or book a school tour.

Why Train at Moorabbin Airport with Goldwings Aviation

Moorabbin Airport is one of the largest aviation education precincts in the world. With five operational runways, 24-hour access, and full air traffic control services, it offers student pilots a truly immersive, real-world flight training experience— whether flying fixed-wing aircraft or rotary helicopters.

Located just 20km from Melbourne CBD, Moorabbin provides the perfect environment for aspiring pilots to develop their skills in controlled airspace. As the saying goes, "If you can fly here, you can fly anywhere."

Goldwings Aviation is strategically located right next to the Moorabbin Airport control tower, giving students fast access to active runways and airside operations. Our flight school offers a dedicated learning environment, enhanced by extra services like a complimentary pickup from the pilot shop and aircraft conveniently parked just steps from the classroom.

We value your time. That’s why Goldwings Aviation at Moorabbin Airport ensures a smooth, efficient training process— from pre-flight briefings to takeoff. Our goal is to make your pilot training both effective and enjoyable.
