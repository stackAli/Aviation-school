Why Choose the Private Pilot Licence (PPL)?

Issued by the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA), the PPL lets you fly further, higher, and with greater flexibility—within Australia and beyond.

It qualifies you to operate more advanced aircraft and perform cross-country flights. Perfect for serious enthusiasts and aspiring commercial pilots alike.

Whether you seek personal adventure or career preparation, this licence opens doors across the aviation world.

How Do You Qualify for the PPL?

  • Be at least 17 years old
  • Log a minimum of 40 flight hours (including solo navigation)
  • Pass the PPL theory exam and in-flight test
  • Hold a valid aviation medical certificate
  • Meet English language proficiency requirements

All PPL training at Moorabbin Airport includes instruction in visual navigation, GPS use, controlled & uncontrolled aerodrome operations.
