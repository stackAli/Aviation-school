Private Pilot Licence (PPL) – Expanding Your Horizons

The PPL is your next step toward greater freedom in the skies. Issued by the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA), it allows you to fly further, higher, and with more flexibility—anywhere in Australia and even internationally.

With a PPL, you can operate a wider range of aircraft and conduct cross-country flights, making it ideal for those seeking more than just local flying.

Whether you're chasing adventure or laying the groundwork for a commercial career, the PPL opens the door to a world of aviation possibilities.

How Do You Qualify for the PPL?

  • Be at least 17 years old
  • Complete a minimum of 40 flight hours (including solo navigation)
  • Pass a PPL theory exam and flight test
  • Hold a valid medical certificate
  • Demonstrate English language proficiency

Training at Moorabbin Airport includes navigation by visual flight rules, GPS and other aids, and exposure to both controlled and non-controlled aerodromes.
