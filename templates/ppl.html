Why Choose the Private Pilot Licence (PPL)?

The Private Pilot Licence (PPL) is your next step toward greater freedom in the skies. Issued by the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA), the PPL allows you to fly further, higher, and with more flexibilityâ€”anywhere in Australia and even internationally.

With a PPL, you can operate a wider range of aircraft and conduct cross-country flights, making it ideal for those seeking more than just local flying.

Whether you're chasing adventure or laying the groundwork for a commercial career, the PPL opens the door to a world of aviation possibilities.

How Do You Qualify for the PPL?

  • Be at least 17 years old
  • Complete a minimum of 40 flight hours (including solo navigation)
  • Pass the PPL theory exam and a CASA flight test
  • Hold a valid medical certificate
  • Demonstrate English language proficiency

Private Pilot Licence (PPL) training at Moorabbin Airport includes experience in both controlled and uncontrolled aerodromes. You'll learn to navigate using Visual Flight Rules (VFR), GPS, and traditional tools to build confidence for solo cross-country flights.
